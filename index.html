Reasons why you should be a Celtics Fan

My name is Jonathan Barrientos and I am a Boston Celtics fan. I grew up in the Boston area and have always considered myself to be a Celtics fan. Even though I started off being a Celtics fan based on where I lived, there are still several reasons why I am a Celtics fan and think you should be too.

For starters, the Celtics hold the record for the most championships with 18 having won their most recent championship in 2024. Out of all the players my favorite player on the team is Jayson Tatum who just recently returned to playing basketball for the Celtics after he ruptured his achilles tendon.

Although the average time to recover from a ruptured achilles is around a year, it only took him 10 months to recover and return to playing at the highest level with the Celtics, immediately playing as a starter and scored 15 points on his first game back.

Not only that but the Celtics were doubted to have a good season missing one of their star players but they pushed through and defied the odds and are now have the 4th best record out of 30 teams this season. These are just a couple reasons on why I am a Boston Celtics fan.
