Famous Players in Celtics History

The Most Legendary Celtics of All Time

Bill Russell: He was a 6'10 Center for the Celtics and played from 1956-1969. He's most known for his record of being a player with the most championship rings in NBA history with 11 rings. His 11 championships make up the majority of the Celtics franchise 18 championships.

Larry Bird: He was a 6'9 Small Forward/Power Forward for the Celtics and played from 1979-1992. He has won three championships with the Celtics and has been known for his three-point shooting (having won 3 three-point shooting contests) and his rivalry with Magic Johnson from the Lakers.

Paul Pierce: He played as a 6'7 Small Forward/Shooting Guard that played for the Boston Celtics from 1998-2013. He won a championship with the Celtics in 2008 alongside well known teammate Kevin Garnett. He's also known for his nickname "The Truth".

Jayson Tatum: The current face of the franchise and one of the top players in the NBA today, who was Drafted 3rd overall by the Boston Celtics in 2018 as a 6'8 Small Forward/Power Forward and is still with the team today. He has a championship win with the Celtics in 2024 alongside also well known teammate and Finals MVP Jaylen Brown.
